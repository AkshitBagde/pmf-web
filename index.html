# Topic Week What You Learn
1 Overview of PM Cases & Interview Formats Week 1 What types of PM interviews exist, how panels evaluate candidates, how to structure answers from day one.
2 Guesstimates Week 1 Frameworks for estimation questions, how to think aloud, avoid common traps, and structure your numbers confidently.

7 1:1 Mock + Resume Review Weeks 4 & 5 1 hour per student with an instructor — full mock interview followed by a resume line-by-line review and personalised feedback.
